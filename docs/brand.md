# CoSign Brand

CoSign is warm, direct, and mutual. The identity treats every collaboration as a small signal passed between two people—not a score, credential, or speculative asset.

## Core line

**Make the moment mutual.**

Use short, human verbs: write, pass, meet, build, help, learn, co-sign. Avoid “reputation protocol,” “trustless,” “immutable proof,” and investment language.

## Mark

The mark combines two open arcs with a shared check. Preserve clear space equal to one quarter of the mark width. Use the full-color mark on Paper; use the light wordmark on Ink. Do not rotate, stretch, recolor individual paths, or place it over noisy imagery.

## Color

| Token | Value | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Paper | `#F5F0E7` | Main background |
| Ink | `#17151F` | Text, outlines, primary action |
| Signal coral | `#FF654A` | Brand energy and pending signals |
| Connection aqua | `#20B8A6` | Mutual/completed state |
| Soft lilac | `#B8A7FF` | Depth and secondary accent |
| Paper soft | `#FFFAF2` | Cards and dialogs |

## Type and layout

Fraunces Variable carries expressive headlines. Manrope Variable carries all interface and body text. Cards use strong Ink outlines, asymmetric lower-left corners, tactile shadows, generous white space, and visible lines between participants.

## Motion

Motion communicates connection: lines fill from creator to signer, checkmarks stamp into place, and cards rise slightly on hover. All motion must collapse under `prefers-reduced-motion`.
